# TrekOps Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to **TrekOps**, the trekking operations, booking, and WhatsApp communication platform available at [trekops.in](https://trekops.in/). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information shared with us through the TrekOps website, app experiences, and connected WhatsApp-based services.

**Last updated:** April 20, 2026

---

## Business and App Identification

This privacy policy belongs to **TrekOps**, available at [https://trekops.in/](https://trekops.in/).

TrekOps is a software platform for trekking operators to manage customer inquiries, bookings, departures, payments, and WhatsApp communications from one system.

If you are reviewing our Meta app or WhatsApp integration, this is the privacy policy for the TrekOps product and the related business services operated under the TrekOps brand.

---

## Information We Collect

TrekOps only collects information voluntarily provided by users, including details shared through WhatsApp, booking inquiries, and use of TrekOps services.

- Name and phone number
- Messages, trek preferences, and inquiry details
- Booking-related details needed to confirm or manage a trek

---

## How We Use Information

Your information is used only to provide TrekOps services and trekking-related customer support.

- Responding to customer inquiries
- Managing trek bookings and confirmations
- Sending customer support updates and reminders
- Improving service quality and communication accuracy

---

## WhatsApp Communication

If you contact TrekOps through WhatsApp or use a TrekOps-connected WhatsApp workflow, we may use the information you share there to continue the conversation, provide booking assistance, send confirmations, and offer customer support related to TrekOps services.

---

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

---

## Data Security

We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect user information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

---

## Data Retention and Deletion Requests

We retain personal information only for as long as needed to respond to inquiries, support bookings, and meet legal or operational requirements. Users may request correction or deletion of their information by contacting us.
