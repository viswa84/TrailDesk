If you contact us on WhatsApp, we may use the information you share there to continue the conversation, provide booking assistance, send confirmations, and offer customer support related to our trekking services.

Data Sharing

Data Security

We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect user information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

We retain personal information only for as long as needed to respond to inquiries, support bookings, and meet legal or operational requirements. Users may request correction or deletion of their information by contacting us.
