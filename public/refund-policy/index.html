Refund Policy

This policy outlines the conditions under which TrekOps offers refunds for subscription plans and platform-related purchases.

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Subscription Refunds

TrekOps subscription fees are generally non-refundable once a billing cycle has begun. However, we offer refunds in the following situations:

  • Cancellation within 7 days of the initial subscription purchase (first-time subscribers only)
  • Significant service outages caused by TrekOps exceeding 48 continuous hours
  • Duplicate charges due to a billing error

Cancellation Timeline

Cancellation Timing Refund Eligibility
Within 7 days of first purchase 100% refund
After 7 days, before renewal No refund — access continues until period ends
After renewal date No refund for the renewed period

Non-Refundable Items

  • Setup fees or onboarding charges
  • WhatsApp Business API usage charges (billed by Meta)
  • Partial months or unused days within a billing cycle
  • Add-on purchases after the 7-day window

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email us at info@trekops.in with the subject line "Refund Request" and include:

  • Your registered email address
  • The reason for the refund request
  • Any relevant transaction or invoice details

Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed within 7–10 business days and returned to the original payment method. Processing times may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.
